# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for College Students
___
### Never   share UPI PIN or OTP: Banks never ask for them via call or message. Ignore urgent "verification" requests.

### Verify before approving UPI requests: Check sender's name and amount; reject unknowns. Use UPI's "collect" feature cautiously.

### Enable UPI transaction limits: Set daily limits (e.g., ₹5,000) in your app settings to cap losses.

### Use strong, unique passwords: Combine letters, numbers, symbols; change them every 3 months. Avoid "123456" or birthdates.

### Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking: Stick to mobile data or trusted networks to prevent snooping.

### Update apps and OS regularly: Enable auto-updates for security patches on Android/iOS.

### Spot phishing emails/SMS: Look for poor grammar, unknown links, or urgent demands. Hover before clicking.

### Report incidents immediately: Use National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or Helpline 1930 for quick action.

### Two-factor authentication everywhere: Activate 2FA on email, social media, and banking apps.

### Educate friends: Share this checklist in your WhatsApp group to build a safer campus community.
